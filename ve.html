How to Delete Your Data

At Best bet, we take your privacy seriously. If you wish to delete your data from our system, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to delete@bestbet.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. In the subject line, please write "Data Deletion Request".
  3. In the body of the email, provide the following information:
    • Your full name
    • Your username
    • A brief description of the data you want to delete
  4. Our team will review your request and process it as soon as possible.
